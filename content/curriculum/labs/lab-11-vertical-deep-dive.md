# Lab 11: Vertical Deep Dive

## Lab Overview

Generic AI strategies fail. In this lab, you'll conduct a comprehensive analysis of a specific vertical and develop an actionable AI playbook tailored to its unique characteristics.

**What you'll create:**
- Vertical landscape analysis
- AI opportunity assessment
- Data and system readiness evaluation
- Competitive intelligence summary
- Complete vertical AI playbook

## Extension Challenge

**Present the Playbook**

1. Create a 10-slide executive presentation summarizing the playbook
2. Practice presenting to an AI assistant playing "skeptical executive"
3. Prepare for questions about:
   - Investment justification
   - Competitive urgency
   - Risk mitigation
   - Team and capability needs
4. Refine based on the Q&A experience
